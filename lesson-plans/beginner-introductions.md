# Beginner Lesson Plan: Introductions

## Level
Beginner (A1)

## Duration
30 minutes

## Objective
Students will be able to introduce themselves and ask simple questions.

## Target Language
- Hello
- My name is ___
- I am from ___
- How are you?

## Lesson Steps

### Warm-up (5 minutes)
- Greet the student
- Ask: “How are you today?”

### Presentation (10 minutes)
- Teach simple introduction sentences
- Use repetition and examples

### Practice (10 minutes)
- Student introduces themselves
- Simple role-play conversation

### Wrap-up (5 minutes)
- Review new words
- Praise student effort
